About Us

A Legacy of Medical Excellence

Founded in 1971, Zeta Beta Mu has been a beacon of brotherhood and professional development for medical practitioners.

Medical Excellence

Our members represent the pinnacle of medical practice across cardiology, neurology, oncology, surgery, and many more specialties.

Brotherhood

A lifelong bond connecting medical professionals across generations, hospitals, and borders — supporting each other from residency to retirement.

Community Service

Through charity missions, free clinics, and health fairs, we give back to the communities that need us the most.

Rich Traditions

Over five decades of traditions, ceremonies, and stories documented in our Traditions Library and Historical Vault.
